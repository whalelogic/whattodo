# WhatToDo in Connecticut

This app uses Gemini API to provide information about things to do in Connecticut. You can ask questions about attractions, events, restaurants, and more.


It has a simple chat UI, and has a 'notebook' feature that allows you to save your favorite activities and places. You can also filter results by location, category, and date.

Locations, places, and events can be pinned to your 'Trip Notes' notebook for easy reference. You can also share your notes with friends or export them for offline use.
